How It Works

Guide

1. Hub — Connect & Configure

Start here. Connect your ESPN account via Firefox cookies, enter your league ID, and load your league. The Hub shows your connection status, Claude API status, and projection freshness.

  • ESPN Login: Extracts cookies from Firefox — you must be logged into ESPN in Firefox first
  • League ID: Found in your ESPN league URL (e.g., 904188626)
  • Generate Projections: Uses Claude to build AI-powered projections (~$0.27)

2. Draft Prep — Plan Every Pick

Walk through your draft round-by-round. The three-panel layout shows your roster (left), player board (center), and war room intel (right).

  • Round Navigator: Click any round dot to jump to that pick
  • Board View: Position columns with tier colors — Elite (red), Solid (blue), Mid (green), Late (gray)
  • Table View: Sortable columns — click headers to sort by AI rank, ADP, SGP, etc.
  • Wishlist: Click the star next to any player to add them to your wishlist
  • Mock Import: Paste ESPN mock draft results to test strategies

3. Draft Live — Real-Time Draft Room

The mission control view for draft day. Polls ESPN every 5 seconds to detect picks. When you're on the clock, a full-screen overlay shows AI recommendations.

  • OTC Strip: Red bar showing pick order — your pick highlighted in gold
  • Auto-Detection: Detects when picks happen and marks players as drafted
  • On-Clock Overlay: Top 4 recommendations with SGP scores and gap analysis
  • Category Scores: Live tracking of your team's category strengths (0-100)

4. War Room — Expert Intelligence

Your daily briefing from AI expert panels. Each expert has a specialty and data source.

  • The Scout: Statcast metrics — barrel%, exit velo, sprint speed, Stuff+
  • The Contrarian: Overvalued/undervalued calls using BABIP, xStats, ADP gaps
  • The Historian: Injury risk, aging curves, spring velocity trends
  • The Quant: Category scarcity, SGP math, league-specific edges
  • Closer Specialist: Bullpen depth charts, committee situations, handcuff targets

5. Intel Center — Data Deep Dives

Raw data and analysis across four tabs:

  • Statcast: Barrel%, exit velocity, sprint speed leaderboards
  • Prospect Watch: Top prospects with ETA and fantasy impact projections
  • Hot Wire: Latest news, injuries, and roster moves affecting fantasy value
  • Two-Starters: Pitchers with two starts next week — streaming gold

6. Understanding the Scores

Category scores (0-100) measure how your team stacks up against championship-level thresholds.

  • Counting stats (R/HR/RBI/SB/K/QS/W/SV): Your projected totals vs. championship team totals
  • Rate stats (AVG/OPS/ERA/WHIP): Your average vs. championship threshold
  • 70+ = championship level — you're competitive in this category
  • Below 40 = critical gap — prioritize this category immediately
  • SGP (Standings Gain Points): How many standings points a player adds — the single best draft metric
  • BUY/AVOID/DEEP tags: AI signals based on Statcast data vs. ADP gap
Keyboard Shortcuts & Tips
Panel Toggles: Click the Roster / Board / War Room buttons to collapse or expand panels
Quick Draft: Click the red "Draft" button next to any player to simulate drafting them in Prep mode
Search: Type in the search box to filter by player name — works in both Table and Board views
